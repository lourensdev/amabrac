Product Info

The starter kit is the quickest and easiest way to get your Ajax alarm system up and running. Once you have installed it yourself, you have the option to add a further 97 devices from the Ajax product range. These include outdoor sensors, sirens, keypads, smart home relays, glass break, fire and leak detectors. The starter kit consists of:

1 x Hub

The Ajax Hub can take up to 100 devices and has up to 15 hours of power from the backup battery. The signal to your smartphone App and monitoring company is sent via the internet and/or sim card. Simply plug the hub into your router with the 1m ethernet cable provided (sim card not included). The Hub can take 50 uses, 9 patricians and connect to 10 cameras.

1 x MotionProtect

The MotionProtect uses a passive infrared sensor to detect human presence. It is "pet-friendly" and ignores cats and dogs with a weight of up to 20 kg and height up to 50 cm.

1 x DoorProtect

The DoorProtect is a wireless opening detector that notifies you of any unauthorized window or door openings. It can be mounted on all types of doors including metal.

1 x SpaceControl

The SpaceControl is a lightweight remote. It has an alarm on, alarm off, stay and panic button. Perfect for lending to guests/employees or use it as a back-up if your phone battery dies.

App

The app sounds a siren on your phone when the alarm goes off. It also essential replaces the old keypads. The App gives you full control of your system. Amongst other features, you can turn the alarm on and off, check the signal and battery strengths, adjust sensor sensitivity and alter the siren volume. With the new SmartSocket, you can even turn on the kettle.
