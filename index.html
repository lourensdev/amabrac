What's in the box:

  • 1 x front plate (to attached to sensors lid)
  • 1 x back plate (to attached to mounting surface). i.e. wall)
  • Instruction leaflet.
  • All nuts, bolts, washes and screws required for mounting.

Product Info

The bracket allows the sensors to be adjusted within a 180-degree angle from the mounting surface. This is useful when the sensor’s detection zone needs to be positioned away from certain vegetation or objects that may cause false alarms. The bracket is powder coated stainless steel for added weather durability. The bracket is also supplied with stainless steel nuts and bolts required for mounting the sensors.
